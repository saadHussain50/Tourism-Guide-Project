Scenic Hill Stations Near Hyderabad

Explore the beautiful retreats around the City of Pearls

Ananthagiri Hills

70 km from Hyderabad

Nestled in the Western Ghats, Ananthagiri Hills is known for its dense forests, ancient caves, and the 400-year-old Anantha Padmanabha Swamy Temple. Perfect for trekking and nature walks.
